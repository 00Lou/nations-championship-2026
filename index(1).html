How it works

Twelve teams are split into a European Conference (the Six Nations) and a Rest of World Conference (Argentina, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, plus invited nations Fiji and Japan). Each team plays all six teams from the opposite conference once, home or away, across the July and November Test windows. When the pool stage ends, every team is ranked within its conference and plays its equivalently-ranked opponent from the other conference during Finals Weekend — so 1st plays 1st in the Grand Final, 2nd plays 2nd, and so on — all staged at Allianz Stadium, Twickenham, from 27–29 November 2026.
